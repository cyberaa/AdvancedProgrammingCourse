Problem G - At the Supermarket

Description

The supermarket close to your place is making a special offer: for each different item that you buy, you receive a bonus amount on your supermarket loyalty card. You can use this bonus to buy other items in the following weeks.

Your plan is to go the supermarket with your uncle's truck and get as much bonus credit as you can. Of course, you only have a certain amount of money to spend, and the truck can only carry so much weight.

Input

The first line indicates the number of test cases. The number of different items n≤160, the maximum weight that the truck can take and the amount of money that you have in your wallet are given in the second line. Then, n lines follow, each of which consisting of three numbers representing the bonus credit, the weight and the price of an item, respectively. Each new test case begins with another number of items, maximum weight and amount of money in a line.

Output

For each test case, your program should output the maximum bonus credit that can be obtained such that the total weight of the chosen items does not exceed the capacity of the truck and that you do not need to pay more than the amount of money that you have in your wallet.

Example

Example input:

1
10 3820 4383
672 677 859
33 228 967
223 79 674
384 506 286
519 994 627
109 511 632
599 378 398
456 65 11
159 604 124
308 542 682

Example output:

3270
